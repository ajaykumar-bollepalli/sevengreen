About Us

Who we are.?

We a small group of people, with identical intention to do good for the needy people. We work for the betterment of needy people by providing basic needs and being a support hand to them. We aim at promoting the welfare of the needy and we strive to put a smile on their face in whatever way we can and hence in turn it'll bring happiness in lot of people.


What we do.?

We stand as volunteers to make the people break their bread happily. There are so many people in this world who struggle to get their basic needs and we want to fulfill the needs for as many as possible. We work "as a family" to relieve the people's suffering. We also motivate others to follow the similar path along with us to serve the society.


What we need.?

We urge the people to come forward and contribute/donate as much as they can so that we can help the people in large numbers. We make sure that your contribution will not go wasted/misused. We want more and more support from everyone so that our channel/group reaches to maximum people. We would be happy to welcome anyone who wish to join us volunteerily to extend our service to the society. Also, we need everyone to let us know if there is any possibility of support needed and we'll be happy to serve the needy.
